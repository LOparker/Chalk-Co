Common questions
Is there a free plan?
No — we don't offer a free tier. Every plan is paid because we're committed to building a best-in-class product, not selling ads or your data. Your 14-day trial gives you full access to try everything risk-free.
Can I switch between monthly and annual?
Yes. Switch anytime from account settings. Annual savings kick in from your next billing cycle. Downgrade to a lower plan anytime too.
What happens after my 14-day trial?
You'll choose a plan and add a card. We'll email you 3 days before your trial ends as a reminder — no surprise charges. Cancel before then and you pay nothing.
Can parents see other students?
Never. Each parent sees only their own child's homework, behavior color, and videos. Privacy is enforced at the account level — it's not a setting that can be turned off.
Do you offer school or district discounts?
Yes — the School plan already includes a per-teacher discount. Contact us for districts buying 20+ seats; we offer custom volume pricing and annual invoicing.
Why no free plan?
Free products monetize through ads, selling data, or neglecting the product. We do none of that. A paid model means we're accountable to you — the teacher — not to advertisers.
